# Pandora AI Traffic & Staffing Optimizer
## 5-Minute Stakeholder Presentation

---

## The Problem (30 seconds)

Retail stores face a critical challenge: **matching staff levels to customer traffic**.

- **Understaffing** during peak hours = lost sales, poor customer experience
- **Overstaffing** during slow periods = wasted labor costs
- **Current systems** use flat, one-size-fits-all schedules that don't adapt to actual traffic patterns

**The result?** We're leaving money on the table every single day.

---

## Our Solution (1 minute)

The **Pandora AI Traffic & Staffing Optimizer** is an AI-powered dashboard that predicts customer traffic and recommends optimal staffing levels in real-time.

### How It Works:
1. **Predicts traffic** hour-by-hour and day-by-day using AI forecasting
2. **Recommends staffing** based on predicted customer visits (1 FTE per 50 visits)
3. **Tracks implementation** - managers commit to either AI or legacy staffing daily
4. **Measures results** - compares forecasted vs. realized staffing efficiency

### Key Innovation:
We don't just provide recommendations - we **track whether managers trust and implement them**, creating accountability and measuring real adoption.

---

## Key Features (1.5 minutes)

### 1. Multi-Store Management
- View all stores at once (London, Copenhagen, Paris) or individually
- Compare performance across locations

### 2. Dual View Modes
- **Hourly View**: 12-hour window for intraday planning (9 AM - 9 PM)
- **Daily View**: 7-day week view for weekly scheduling

### 3. Smart Forecasting
- **Pink line**: AI-predicted customer traffic
- **Dark line**: Actual historical traffic (for validation)
- **Manual adjustments**: Store managers can tweak predictions based on local insights

### 4. Staffing Recommendations
- **Grey bars**: Legacy/baseline staffing (flat schedule)
- **Green bars**: AI-recommended staffing (dynamic)
- Clear comparison showing average FTE per day and revenue impact

### 5. Implementation Tracking (Our Secret Weapon)
- Managers commit daily: "Following AI" or "Using Legacy"
- **30-day calendar heatmap** shows adoption history
- **Adoption metrics**: Today, 3-day, and weekly rates
- **Regional view**: Identify high-performers and stores needing support
